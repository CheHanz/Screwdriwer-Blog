What I'm Looking For

  • Vintage sets: Pre-1960s complete sets in original cases
  • Unusual materials: Bone, ivory, exotic wood handles
  • Military issue: Especially WWII or Cold War era
  • Artisan pieces: Handcrafted or artistically designed
  • Prototypes: Experimental or one-off designs
  • Branded tools: Company promotional sets

Even if your screwdrivers don't fit these categories, I'd still love to see them! Every tool has a story worth preserving.
